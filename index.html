About DK Metals

Your trusted partner for premium metal exports from the United Kingdom

DK Metals is a Cambridge-based metal trading company specialising in the export of high-quality ferrous and non-ferrous metals to international markets. As a licensed scrap metal dealer, we maintain the highest standards of compliance and quality assurance in all our operations.

With our strategic location and extensive network of suppliers across the UK, we source premium grade metals and deliver them efficiently to clients worldwide. Our commitment to transparency, competitive pricing, and reliable service has made us a preferred partner for businesses seeking quality metal supplies.
